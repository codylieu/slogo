Basic Syntax

Command Description
fd Moves turtle pixels forward
bk Moves turtle pixels backward
lt Turns turtle counterclockwise by degrees angle
rt Turns turtle clockwise by degrees angle
seth Turns turtle to absolute heading in degrees
goto Moves turtle to absolute position in pixels
pd Puts the pen down, turtle movement will leave a trail
pu Puts the pen up, turtle movement will not leave a trail
st Shows turtle (visible)
ht Hides turtle (invisible)
home Moves turtle to center of screen
cs Clears screen, removes turtle trail and sends it to home position
